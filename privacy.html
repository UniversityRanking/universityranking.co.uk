Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit UniversityRanking.co.uk.

1. Who We Are

UniversityRanking.co.uk is an independent, UK-based website that ranks universities based on student satisfaction data.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Basic technical data (IP address, browser type, device type)
  • Analytics data (e.g., pages visited, time on site) via Google Analytics
  • Cookies and advertising identifiers via Google AdSense
  • Emails you send us directly (if you contact us)

3. Why We Collect Data

  • To improve the performance and usability of the site
  • To serve relevant advertising and measure its effectiveness
  • To respond to feedback and improve content accuracy

4. Use of Cookies and Third-Party Services

We use cookies to personalise content and ads, provide social media features, and analyse traffic. Third parties such as Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to show you personalised ads based on your browsing behaviour.
